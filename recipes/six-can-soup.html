Six Can Soup

Description

This is an incredibly easy-to-make beef taco soup. I learned this recipe from a cool mom who had learned it from someone else.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chili with no beans
  • 1 can whole kernel corn
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 can vegetable soup
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes or diced tomatoes
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • Some grated chees: mild cheddar, Mexican blend, or the like
  • 1 bag of Fritos

All cans are standard size. For a milder variant of this soup, halve or omit the diced tomatoes with green chilies.

Steps

  1. Empty contents of all cans into a soup pot and mix together. Do not drain or reconstitute anything; just dump them in.
  2. Heat through on medium to high temperature, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat.
  3. Serve as follows: put a handful of Fritos in each bowl. Pour soup on the Fritos, and then sprinkle grated cheese on top. I recommend milk to drink with this soup.
